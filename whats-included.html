Yes, 100% free. There are no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no paywalls. Every module, quiz, and resource is available at no cost — forever.

Who is Hawk Academy for?

Everyone! Whether you're a complete beginner, a marketing professional, a small business owner, or someone looking to upskill — Hawk Academy is designed for all levels.

How long does it take to finish?

You learn at your own pace. Some students complete it in a few weeks, others take a few months. There are no deadlines or time limits.

Absolutely. SEO is one of the most in-demand digital marketing skills. Completing Hawk Academy gives you practical, real-world skills that employers value.

Do I need any prior SEO knowledge?

Not at all. Hawk Academy starts from the basics and builds up. It's designed so that anyone can follow along, regardless of experience level.

Can I ask questions if I get stuck?

Yes! You can use the Hawk Academy community forums and LinkedIn group to ask questions, get help, and connect with other learners.

Is the content up to date?

Yes. We update Hawk Academy regularly to reflect the latest Google algorithm changes, AI search developments, and industry best practices.

Can I come back and review the content later?

Of course! Your progress is saved and you can revisit any module at any time. We encourage reviewing content as the industry evolves.
