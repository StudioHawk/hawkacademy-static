Google Trust Scanner

See what Google actually reads on your website

Topic Coverage

7 pages found covering 4 distinct topics. Most successful sites in your space cover 15+ topics with dedicated pages for each.

Content Depth

Average 180 words per page. Google needs 500+ words to understand your expertise. Most of your pages read like brochures, not resources.

Site Structure

Some internal links found but no clear topic clusters. Google can't tell which pages are your most important or how topics connect.

Trust Signals

No team page, no reviews on site, generic about page. Google looks for proof that real people run your business. Right now, it can't find any.

Biggest Gap

Your pages don't have enough content for Google to trust you as an expert. Most pages are under 200 words -- that's a caption, not a page. Until you give Google enough text to understand what you actually do, your competitors with longer, more detailed pages will always rank above you.

Fix This Week

1 Pick your most important service page and expand it to 500+ words. Add what the service includes, who it's for, common questions, and what makes you different.
2 Add a proper "About" page with real names, photos, qualifications, and your story. Google uses this as a trust signal.
3 Add 3 customer reviews directly on your homepage. Not a link to Google Reviews -- put the actual text on the page so Google can read it.
