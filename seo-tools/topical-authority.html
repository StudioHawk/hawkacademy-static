How to export your data (2 min)

Search Console (required)

  1. Open Google Search Console and pick your site.
  2. Performance → Search results.
  3. Date range: Last 3 months.
  4. Make sure all 4 metrics are on: Clicks, Impressions, CTR, Position.
  5. Click the Queries tab → Export ↓ → CSV.
  6. Open the ZIP, find Queries.csv, upload below.

Keyword list (optional)

  1. Any of: Semrush, Ahrefs, Mangools, Ubersuggest, Keywords Everywhere, or your own list.
  2. We read columns named anything close to keyword + volume. Difficulty is optional.
  3. Or just paste one keyword per line (no volume needed).
