Get a Calculator for Your Website

Your customers type things like "how much does a plumber cost" into Google every day. A little calculator on your site answers that question — and gets you found on Google. Here's how to add one in 10 minutes. No tech skills needed.

First — what's a "calculator page"?

It's a little interactive tool on your website. The customer types in a few things (the size of their job, their budget, their zip code) and it gives them an answer — a price estimate, a quote, a timeline, or a comparison.

Think of a tradie site with an "emergency callout cost calculator," a mortgage broker with a "repayments calculator," a gym with a "personal training cost calculator." Simple. Useful. Takes 2 minutes to use.

Why bother putting one on your site?

📈
Google traffic People search "how much does X cost" every day. Calculator pages rank for those searches and bring you free traffic.
📞
More enquiries Once someone sees a rough number, they're far more likely to pick up the phone or fill in your form.
🤝
Trust & authority Showing real numbers makes you look like the expert. Customers pick the business that's clear and upfront.
