How to export your data from Google Search Console (2 min)
  1. Open Google Search Console and pick your site.
  2. Click Performance → Search results in the left sidebar.
  3. Set the date range to Last 3 months (top right).
  4. Make sure all 4 metrics are turned on at the top: Total clicks, Total impressions, Average CTR, Average position.
  5. Scroll down to the table, click the Queries tab.
  6. Click the Export ↓ button (top right of the table) → Download CSV.
  7. Open the downloaded ZIP, find Queries.csv and either upload it below or open it and copy-paste the contents.
